🇭🇳 Honduras  ·  Bethesda, MD

"I build things that haven't been built yet — from prosthetic hands to spacecraft that return from Mars."

My name is David Reina, a student at Walt Whitman High School in Bethesda, Maryland. Originally from Honduras, I carry a perspective shaped by two worlds — and a drive to engineer solutions that matter across both of them.

In the summer of 2025 I attended the Rice Aerospace Academy at Rice University, where my team designed a conceptual 5-stage Mars Sample Return Lander — one of the most ambitious challenges in modern space exploration.

My work spans 3D modeling, CAD design, and propulsion analysis. I've also competed internationally — representing Honduras at a Shanghai sustainable city design camp with students from 10+ countries.
